\section{Decision condition}

The Fase~8 work is \emph{not} required because:

\begin{itemize}
  \item Cap.~89 closes the XFEM small-strain gate at
        \(\mathrm{NZ}\in\{4,5,6,8\}\) up to 300~mm cyclic amplitude.
  \item Cap.~89 closes the corotational gate at 200/300~mm.
  \item TL/UL residuals are recorded with a documented narrow
        peak-ratio overshoot at 200~mm and a larger overshoot at
        300~mm; this is honest research evidence, not a blocker for
        the FE\(^2\) chain (Cap.~90) which uses small-strain.
\end{itemize}

Fase~8 would only be activated if a future Phase~6 multiscale
claim required a non-enriched fallback for sites where XFEM
activation is not justified -- e.g. low-damage transition columns in
the L-shape where the enrichment overhead is not warranted.
